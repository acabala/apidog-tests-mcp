# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Give feedback on code, not people.
- Help keep discussions focused and inclusive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or block participants for violations.

Report behavior concerns to:

- `adrian.cabala@loftyworks.com`
